Think like a machine!

We have a machine that can look at a picture and tell us what it is. Most of the time, it tells us the right answers. But sometimes it gives surprising answers. We don't know if this is because the machine gets confused, or if it's because the machine noticed some object or pattern hidden in the image. We've collected some of these surprising answers here, and we want to know whether you can guess what answer the machine made.

On each trial, you'll see an image that will look to you like a nonsense collection of colors and shapes. But the machine looked at these images and thought they were real objects. For each image, we want you to take your best guess at what the machine thought these images were.

When you are ready to begin, click "Start Experiment". By completing this survey, you are consenting to be in this research study. Your participation is voluntary and you can stop at any time.
